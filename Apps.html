Applications

There are several mobile apps designed specifically to help the environment and encourage more sustainable practices. These apps offer a variety of functions, from educating about environmental issues to providing tools for leading a more eco-friendly lifestyle.

App list

  • JouleBug: Promotes sustainable and environmentally friendly lifestyle habits through challenges and achievements. JouleBug
  • Good On You: Evaluates the sustainability of fashion brands and helps users make informed decisions about their purchases. Good On You
  • Too Good To Go: Fight against food waste by connecting users with establishments that sell surplus food at reduced prices. Too Good To Go
  • Oroeco: Track your carbon footprint and offer suggestions to reduce your environmental impact. Oroeco
  • RecycleNation: Helps users find nearby places where they can recycle different types of materials. RecycleNation
  • Forest: Motivates users to stay focused on their work by planting a virtual tree. If you are successful, the tree grows; if you get distracted, the tree dies. Forest
  • Green Gold: Encourages reforestation and allows users to "adopt" trees in deforested areas. Green Gold
  • EcoChallenge: Provides daily challenges to help people adopt more sustainable and conscious habits. EcoChallenge
  • Plastic Patrol: Helps combat plastic pollution by documenting and removing plastic debris on beaches and bodies of water. Plastic Patrol
  • Yuka: Scan food and cosmetic products to assess their impact on health and the environment. Yuka
