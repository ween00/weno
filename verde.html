Inspiring case studies

companies that have managed to reduce their carbon footprint through sustainable programming practices.

Examples

  1. Etsy: Etsy, an e-commerce platform, implemented a strategy called "Renewable Electricity" to reduce its environmental impact. They have optimized their technology infrastructure and have worked on the efficient design of their servers to improve energy efficiency. In addition, they have invested in renewable energy to power their operations and data centers.
  2. Microsoft Project Natick: Microsoft has carried out an innovative project called Natick, in which they developed underwater data centers to reduce energy consumption for cooling. Submerging data centers in the ocean reduces cooling costs and uses natural resources to maintain the proper temperature.
  3. Google: Google has been committed to sustainability in programming and in its infrastructure of cloud services. They have implemented energy optimization algorithms in their data centers and have worked on the energy efficiency of their services to reduce resource consumption.
  4. Microsoft: Microsoft has been focused on reducing its carbon footprint and has announced plans to be "carbon negative" by 2030. They have explored new ways to design and run data centers more efficiently and are investigating how scheduling can contribute to a greater sustainability.
  5. Uber Movement: Uber launched the "Uber Movement" platform that uses trip data to help cities make more informed transportation planning decisions. This can result in a reduction in traffic and in the emission of greenhouse gases.
